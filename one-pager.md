## The Solution

**WhatsApp AI Lead Automation**

A 24/7 system that:
- Responds to every lead instantly
- Qualifies them automatically (budget, needs, timeline)
- Scores them: Hot / Warm / Cold
- Alerts your sales team only when it matters
- Tracks everything in your CRM

**Your team stops chasing. Starts closing.**

## How It Works

1. **Lead messages your WhatsApp**
2. **AI responds in seconds** — 24/7, natural conversation
3. **AI qualifies** — asks the right questions, collects info
4. **Lead gets scored** — Hot (ready now), Warm (needs nurture), Cold (not a fit)
5. **Hot lead → instant alert** to your sales team with full context
6. **Everything logged** in your CRM automatically
